Student Reporting View Reports

Banner Operational Data Store
Reporting View Target: ENROLLMENT

Description Contains enrollment data for those persons with registration activity in an academic period. Note the persons permitted to register who may or may not have a row in ENROLLMENT can be obtained using the STUDENT reporting view. All active and permitted to register persons who have program or curriculum will have a row in the ACADEMIC STUDY reporting view.
Key & Frequency One row per person per academic period enrolled.
Recommended Search Columns PERSON_UID, ACADEMIC_PERIOD
